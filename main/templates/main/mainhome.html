Vikrama Simhapuri University Nellure

No 1 COLLEGE

Vikrama Simhapuri University Nellure, Nellore, in its seminal role as a higher institute is grooming the careers of the youth to create a think-tank of resourcefulness for macro stakes in nation-building. The University is currently offering eleven courses keeping in view the resources of the region and the employability of the graduates. The University was established by Act No. 29/2008 enacted by the Legislature of the State of Andhra Pradesh on 25th June 2008. It started offering six Postgraduate courses of contemporary relevance from August 2008. Research programmes leading to the award of the Ph.D. degree have been launched on 29.08.2010.

2 nd PLACE

Prof. P. Venkata Rao , Department of Commerce, VSU PG Centre, Kavali was appointed Dean, College Development Council. He was born on 01-04-1959 at Chimakurthi village, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh. He is the elder son of Sri.Venkateswarlu garu and Smt.Adilakshamma garu. He completed his education from 1st class to Degree at Ongole town. Later he joined at S.V.University P.G.Centre Kavali and completed his M.Com.,M.Phil.,and Ph.D.,.He also completed B.Ed., P.G.D.C.M.P., and MBA courses from reputed educational institutions. He has attended for the Training Programme in Applied Rural Banking and Short-term course in Research Methods in social sciences.
